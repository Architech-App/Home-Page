Plate

Democratizing dining for all.

What Plate does

Plate is a personalized guide to dining for those with dietary restrictions or food allergies. Free and easy-to-use, Plate provides valuable peer-based ratings reflecting how well (or poorly) restaurants accommodate your dietary needs and preferences. This sharing of real experiences from other diners in your same diet community allows you to quickly and easily find restaurants that cater best to your specific dietary needs.

Who Plate serves

Plate currently supports the Vegetarian, Pescatarian, Swaminarayan, and Kosher diets in New York City. We plan to support all major diets & other locations in the coming months! If you're interested in the product but Plate does not currently serve your dietary restrictions & location, please sign-up for the waitlist below and we'll reach out when Plate is ready for you.
